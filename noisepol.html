What is noise pollution?


Noise pollution is annoying or harmful levels of noise that can cause disorders,other health related issues, and even hearing loss.


What causes noise pollution?


There are many things that cause noise pollution, but here are 2 of the most causing


Vehicles

Vehicles such as cars, trucks, airplanes cause the most noise pollution and can badly harm people who experince this kind of noise a lot.


Things such as loudspeakers, inustrial machines, and animals also cause noise pollution

Noise pollution is bad, but is there some way to reduce it?


Yes! here are a list of ways to lessan noise pollution and to keep away from it

  • Keep doors shut when using noisy machinery such as vacuum or blender
  • Use headphones when playing any media in public
  • other little things like keeping volume low or staying away from noisy area
